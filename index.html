Our Products

Butter cookies

Handcrafted Butter Cookies, Baked to Perfection

Delight in our rich, buttery cookies, baked to perfection with premium ingredients. Each bite offers a melt-in-your-mouth experience that's simply irresistible. Perfect for any occasion or a sweet treat just for you.

Cashew Nut Cookies

Crunchy, Nutty Delight in Every Bite

Savor the rich, buttery flavor of our Cashew Nut Cookies. Loaded with crunchy cashew pieces and baked to golden perfection, these cookies offer a delightful blend of textures and tastes. Ideal for a satisfying snack or a thoughtful gift.

Choco Oats Cookies

Decadent Chocolate and Oats in Perfect Harmony

Our multigrain loaf is a hearty and flavorful blend of wheat, oats, flaxseed, and sunflower seeds. This bread offers a satisfying crunch with every bite and is rich in dietary fiber.

Chocochip Cookies

Irresistible Chocolate Chips in Every Bite

Enjoy our Chocochip Cookies, packed with gooey chocolate chips and baked to perfection. Each cookie offers a sweet, chocolatey crunch that's hard to resist. Perfect for satisfying your sweet cravings.

peanut Cookies

Rich, Nutty Flavor in Every Bite

Savor the creamy richness of our Peanut Butter Cookies, loaded with roasted peanuts for extra crunch. Each bite delivers a perfect balance of nutty goodness and sweetness, making them a delightful treat for any time of day.

Usmaniya Cookies

Traditional Flavor, Timeless Appeal

Enjoy the timeless taste of Usmaniya Cookies, featuring a blend of traditional spices and a perfect crunch. A delightful treat that combines rich flavor with a touch of nostalgia.
